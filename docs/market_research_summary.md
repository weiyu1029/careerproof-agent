# Market Research Summary

## Research stage

This is early qualitative discovery based on survey responses from graduate students, recent graduates, and working professionals preparing for business, data, operations, product, strategy, consulting, and analytics roles.

## Main insight

Candidates do not only need more interview answers. They need help understanding what a hiring manager is evaluating and how to turn real experience into credible evidence.

## Repeated pain points

- Not knowing what the hiring manager is really evaluating.
- Not knowing how to connect past experience to a target role.
- Difficulty understanding unfamiliar industries before interviews.
- Difficulty preparing business cases, analytics cases, SQL / technical screens, and structured answers.
- Frustration with generic AI-generated answers that are not role-specific or industry-specific.

## Most desired product features

- Experience-to-interview-story translator.
- Business model explanation by industry.
- Key metrics by industry.
- Role-specific interview questions and answer frameworks.
- Resume bullet examples by industry.
- Questions to ask interviewers.
- SQL / analytics practice by role.
- 30-minute interview preparation roadmap.

## Product implication

CareerProof should position itself as an evidence-based career intelligence agent, not a generic answer generator.

> Generic AI gives answers. CareerProof builds evidence.
