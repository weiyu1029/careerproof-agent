# CareerProof Product Strategy

## One-line positioning

CareerProof is a personal AI career concierge that turns job descriptions into evidence-based interview strategy.

## Wedge product

The first wedge is job-description-to-interview-strategy for business, data, BI, product, operations, strategy, consulting, and AI/data product candidates.

## Product pillars

1. Personal Career Intelligence Graph
2. Hiring Signal Intelligence Database
3. Industry Interview Knowledge Graph
4. Interview Story Bank
5. Gap Analysis and Prep Roadmap
6. Coach / Mock Interview Marketplace
7. University Career Center Version
8. API / Agent Marketplace Version

## Differentiation

Most AI tools help candidates polish text. CareerProof helps candidates prove fit.

## Ideal customer profile

- Graduate students applying to business/data/ops roles
- International students entering unfamiliar industries
- Career switchers
- Business analytics and data analytics candidates
- University career centers
- Career coaches and mock interviewers

## Monetization path

- Free JD breakdown
- Paid interview strategy pack
- Mock interview marketplace take rate
- University / career center subscription
- API / agent marketplace integrations
