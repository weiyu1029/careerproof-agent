# Sample Output: FinTech Risk Analyst

## Role Problem Map

This role is likely focused on balancing growth and risk: approving more qualified users while controlling default, fraud, and repayment risk.

## Key Metrics

Approval rate, default rate, fraud rate, repayment rate, loss rate, credit utilization, CAC, and LTV.

## Candidate Translation

Operations analytics and dashboard work can transfer into risk monitoring, especially around anomaly detection, process improvement, and stakeholder-facing reporting. The candidate should prepare a story showing how they diagnosed a metric change and recommended an operational intervention.
