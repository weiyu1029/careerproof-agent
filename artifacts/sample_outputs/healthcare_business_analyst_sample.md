# Sample Output: Healthcare Business Analyst

## Role Problem Map

This role is likely hired to solve a healthcare operations visibility problem. The company needs dashboards and KPI definitions that help stakeholders understand patient access, provider utilization, wait times, reporting gaps, and operational bottlenecks.

## Industry Context

Healthcare operations teams care about patient wait time, no-show rates, provider utilization, claim denials, appointment availability, and patient satisfaction. A strong candidate should understand both analytics and stakeholder communication in a regulated environment.

## Hiring Evidence Matrix

| Hiring signal | Candidate evidence | Confidence | Risk |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Dashboarding | Power BI dashboard work improving decision efficiency by 30% | Strong | Add business context |
| Operations analytics | Uber operations and complaint rate reporting | Strong | Translate to healthcare workflow |
| Stakeholder communication | Cross-functional work with Product, Sales, CRM, PR, Legal | Strong | Prepare one clear story |
| SQL / data work | Analytics experience mentioned | Medium | Add SQL example if available |

## Interview Story Bank

1. Building decision infrastructure with dashboards
2. Reducing complaint rate through operational reporting
3. Coordinating cross-functional ERP implementation
4. Translating operations ambiguity into metrics

## Gap Analysis

The candidate should prepare healthcare-specific metrics and examples of how operations analytics can transfer from mobility / food delivery into healthcare access and clinic operations.
